Driven by passion to create benchmarks in the field of healthcare, ADk Medicare was formed in 1985 as a modest 22 bed facility. We had a dream to meet the growing healthcare needs of people in the region and a mission to make superior healthcare facilities available to the masses at affordable cost. With the blessings of the Almighty and the relentless commitment by a team of professionals along with following ethical practices in every realm of healthcare and the unstinted support and patronage of our patients, Adk Medicare is now a multi specialty, tertiary care, referral hospital, running as a Public Limited Company.
